Detailed Information

Appearance
When it comes to her physique, she’s fairly average in height and weight, but has kept herself purposefully well toned. Natalie cares a great deal about her own appearance, and has kept her body as close to her own idea of perfection as possible, working to keep a thin layer of muscle under her hourglass curves. Speaking of curves, her feminine lines are very distinct, especially around the chest and rear. Natalie’s complexion is very pale, or at least is given the illusion of being as such, since very dark naturally black hair covers her head. Her eyes are a pale grey, adding no real color to her already cold face. The structure of her features are mostly angular, high sharp cheekbones working with a strong jaw and full pink lips making up for the lack of maturity displayed in her behavior.

Personality
Crude, selfish, and arrogant are only a few of the many negative words that can be used to describe her personality. Ultimately, she behaves like a spoiled brat, prone to throwing tantrums fit for toddlers despite being a fully grown woman. She is also incredibly manipulative, constantly looking for ways to play people and things to her own advantage. Natalie’s primary focus is always on herself, even if she is defending or willingly working with--or for--another person, the core motive can always be traced back to her own benefit. Fully aware of her own amassed fortune, she never misses a good opportunity to flaunt it.

Weapons & Gear

Leftie & Rightie
Two mechanized gauntlets she wears has can shoot up small dart-like projectiles, she calls thorns. These are made of a special material produced by a recently acquired company. More or less, it is one of the few things that can survive a black hole.

DIY Galaxy Skin Deluxe
The source of her suit is an exo-skeletal spinal implant that starts along the spine below the shoulder blades and stretches a foot down her back before abruptly ending. When activated, it secretes a gel-like matter that is designed to spread over the surface of her flesh until her entire body from head to toe is completely covered. Upon reaching completion, it changes from an almost-liquid to a stretchy breathable solid that is thin enough for her to draw oxygen through despite the fact that it covers her mouth and nose. The sections that cover her eyes are akin to one-way windows, acting almost like sunglasses when peering out from the inside, but visual from the outside doesn't allow the same effect to the inside. The "fabric" of the entire getup acts like a screen, projecting visuals of the vast cosmos.
The suit has an ability that works well in tandem with Cosmic's skills. Much like a black hole, it can draw physical objects and energies toward itself, and if physical contact is made it will break down and take apart what it comes in contact with for the sake of collecting information. This information is in turn transmitted directly to its wearer's mind, allowing her to understand the inner workings and processes of other people's possessions or abilities. Contact is required for this to work, and the larger the source of energy or physical object is, the longer amount of time physical contact will need to be continuously upheld for the "breakdown" to be effective.
This ability can be overwhelmed as the suit is limited to collecting information from only up to two separate sources at a time. If it makes contact with too many different forms of energies/objects from completely different sources while in the middle of gathering information its processes will be overridden and severe mental backlash will give Cosmic an intense focus breaking migraine.

Appearance

Cosmic looks like a galaxy themed anomaly. There are no visible defining facial features on her, she simply appears to be a piece of outer space shaped into a very curvy feminine silhouette. Any clothing she chooses to wear is usually simplistic, consisting of little more than an open scifi-esque trench coat, elbow length gloves, thigh length stockings, and practical boots.

Personality

While her appearance alters drastically between identities, the only thing about her personality that changes is how easily she devolves into a tantrum. Cosmic is a lot less likely to behave like a child--at least, when people are watching--but is still just as selfish, cold, and crude as Natalie. In her alternate persona, she reflects more of a mature air, but her motives remain the same.
